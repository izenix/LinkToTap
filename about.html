about hurst

Welcome to Linktotap, your trusted partner in NFC (Near Field Communication) technology. We specialize in designing and delivering innovative NFC card solutions that bridge the gap between the digital and physical worlds. Our cutting-edge technology empowers businesses and individuals to seamlessly share information, simplify transactions, and enhance connectivity with just a tap.

Our Mission

At Linktotap, our mission is to revolutionize the way people interact with technology by harnessing the power of NFC. We aim to provide smart, secure, and scalable solutions that elevate user experiences while fostering efficiency and innovation.

What We Offer

We offer a wide range of NFC-enabled products and services tailored to meet diverse industry needs:

  • Smart Business Cards: Redefine networking with customizable NFC business cards that instantly share your contact details.
  • Event Solutions: Streamline check-ins, ticketing, and access control for events.
  • Retail & E-commerce: Enable cashless payments and effortless customer engagement with NFC-integrated solutions.
  • Custom NFC Solutions: Bespoke NFC card solutions tailored to your unique requirements.

Why Choose Linktotap?

  • Innovation: We stay ahead of the curve by leveraging the latest advancements in NFC technology.
  • Quality: Our products are crafted with precision, ensuring durability and seamless functionality.
  • Customer-Centric: We prioritize your needs, offering personalized support and consultation to create the perfect solution.
  • Security: Our technology ensures the highest standards of data protection and compliance.

Our Vision

We envision a world where technology enhances connections, making everyday interactions simpler, smarter, and more meaningful. By leveraging NFC technology, we aim to bring this vision to life, one tap at a time.
